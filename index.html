How I Work

As of writing this, I have no previous experience as a designer. However, I am currently taking a UI design course (SEG 3125) taught by Caroline Barrière. The course consists of "Psychological principles of human-computer interaction. Evaluation of user interfaces. Usability engineering. Task analysis, user-centered design and prototyping. Conceptual models and metaphors. Software design rationale. Design of windows, menus and commands. Voice and natural language I/O. Response time and feedback. Colour, icons and sound. Internationalization and localization. User interface architectures and APIs. Case studies and project."

As part of the course, we regularly use resources from the NN/g (Nielsen and Norman Group) and Medium to deepen our understanding of industry best practices and design principles.
